Privacy
Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Lynarch Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full Name — to address you correctly in our response.
  • Email Address — to reply to your enquiry.
  • Phone Number — if you choose to provide it, for follow-up calls.
  • Project Details / Message — to understand your requirements.

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes beyond standard hosting analytics. See our Cookies Policy for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. Specifically:

  • To reply to questions about our services.
  • To follow up on project discussions you have initiated.
  • To schedule studio meetings or site visits if requested.

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing communications. We may occasionally reach out about project updates if you are an existing client.

3. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Formspree (formspree.io), a form processing service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers before being delivered to us. Formspree's privacy policy can be found at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Our website is hosted on standard web hosting infrastructure. Hosting providers may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not control this data.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

Lynarch Studio does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. Your data is shared only with the service providers listed above, and only as necessary to deliver our services.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry data for as long as necessary to respond to your request and maintain business records. If you would like your data deleted, please contact us at info@lynarchstudio.com and we will remove it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw any consent you have given at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@lynarchstudio.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All form submissions are transmitted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
