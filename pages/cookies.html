This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how Lynarch Studio uses them on this website, and how you can control your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (which expire when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

2. How We Use Cookies

The Lynarch Studio website is a minimal portfolio site. We aim to keep our use of cookies as lean as possible. Here is what may be in use:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the website. They do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled:

  • Session management — standard browser session data to ensure pages load correctly.

Analytics Cookies (if enabled)

If we use an analytics tool (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-friendly alternative like Plausible), these cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site — for example, which pages are most visited and how long visitors stay. This information is collected in aggregate and does not personally identify you.

Analytics cookies we may use include:

  • _ga, _gid (Google Analytics) — track page views and visitor behaviour anonymously.
  • plausible (Plausible Analytics) — privacy-first analytics with no personal data collection.

If you prefer not to be tracked by analytics, you can opt out using browser settings (see Section 4 below) or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Cookies

Some content or functionality on this website may be provided by third parties (such as embedded maps or fonts loaded from Google Fonts). These third parties may set their own cookies. Lynarch Studio does not control these cookies and they are subject to the respective third party's privacy policies.

  • Google Fonts — may log a request when fonts are loaded. No personal data is retained. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — our contact form processor may use minimal cookies for spam prevention. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

3. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not use cookies for advertising or retargeting.
  • We do not build personal profiles based on your browsing behaviour.
  • We do not sell or share cookie data with third parties for commercial gain.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. Here are links to instructions for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of this website. Disabling cookies is unlikely to significantly impact your experience on the Lynarch Studio website, as we do not rely on them for core functionality.

Opt-out tools: You can also opt out of analytics tracking using tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.
