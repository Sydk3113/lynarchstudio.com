"Architecture should not only serve its purpose — it should tell a story. We don't just design buildings; we craft immersive experiences that stand the test of time."

Lynarch Studio is an architecture and interior design practice based in Chennai, working across residential, hospitality, and commercial projects in India and the Gulf. Founded on February 16, 2024, by Syed Kuraib and Amreen N, the studio was built on a shared vision of creating thoughtful spaces where design is rooted in intention, context, and human experience.

We challenge conventional norms — embracing innovation while respecting tradition. Every project begins with a careful understanding of context, climate, and the people who inhabit the space, with strong emphasis on proportion, light, materiality, and craftsmanship.
